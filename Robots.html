Who is the world's smartest robot?

Sophia the robot, an AI robot invented in 2016 has been dubbed the smartest robot. It is capable of answering questions and making human-like facial expressions. It uses AI to analyze conversation and then respond to the question. It costs about 50,000 dollars to buy. The robot also has a citizenship of Saudi Arabia. Hanson Robotics, the company that invented Sophia, has planned on inventing more of the same robots to help in the pandemic.
